M&C GRODIE FARMS

Dittmer, Missouri

Starting with a small herd of Angus cattle in 2012, their farm has grown to a robust head of 300 spanning dozens of paddocks. Fans of 100% Angus meat appreciate the delicacy and tenderness of each juicy bite. Matt and Courtney perfected the product that their waitlist is for cattle yet to be born. Getting trained in artificial insemination, Courtney started breeding their pure Angus heifers with high quality, Japanese Wagyu sperm. The offspring of a portion of their herd produces what is called Wangus beef, marrying the name of combined breeds Wagyu and Angus. In 2019, the farm delivered their first Wangus calf.
