M&C GRODIE FARMS

Dittmer, Missouri

M&C Grodie Farms is a family run farm in Dittmer, Missouri since 2012.


Growing up blocks a part, in the suburbs of St. Louis County their paths rarely crossed; not until a chance encounter Courtney’ senior year of high school. During the reception of a mutual friend’s wedding, Matt asked Courtney to dance. Five years later they were married, and they scoured the surrounding areas for land to start a farm. Visiting a near perfect property in Dittmer left them with the realty of farm ownership. Spanning over 150 acres, Lone Elm Farm is surrounded by forests, creeks, and a beautiful, white home built with the hands of its dedicated owners. Matt and Courtney could see their future before their eyes. Archiving the success of M&C Grodie Farms will not only benefit communicating their story, but it will also document its journey for their future generations of farmers.
